our games facilitate

Collaborative Play

Face-to-face play with the screen as a shared focus is a completely different learning experience. The kids will improve team work, social as well as fine motor skills and coordination.

Relevant Learning

Our games draw on a wide range of skills, pertaining to math, language, art, story telling and logic thinking, applicable in all classes.

Scalable difficulty

Game settings can easily be adjusted so the level of difficulty will scale to and match any age or level of mastery from kindergarten to primary school.

Designed by teachers for teachers

Our design philosophy is based on input from professionals and an experienced need for alternative learning methods for the digital generation.
